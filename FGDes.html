Fox Glacier

Fox Glacier is named after Sir William Fox, New Zealand's prime minister from 1869 to 1872. It decends into temperate rainforest just 300 meters above the sea level. The sights and actitives are beautiful for an adventure with the reflection of the mountians above Lake Matherson. Aside from the general activities you can also take a helicopter 30 min flight over the glacier.
You can also take a short walk around the rocky terrain. This walk can take from 30 minutes to an hour one way. Just make sure to be carful of the loose rocks! There is also ice-hiking events and scenic flights. This glacier isnt way up in the mountians either, they are close to lake Matheson on Cook Flat Rock which is near a town that offers a good choice of cafes and restaurants.

Activities



 

Skydive Fox

Voted 2nd most senic skydive tour in the world, but top in New Zealand. The skydive fox glacier airfield is located in the heart of Fox Glacier village within walking distance to hotels and inns. Here you will skydive from 16,500ft above the West Coast glaciers.The average price is $299-$399

 Fox it up: Heli Ice Climb Adventure

 Climb and walk up the World Famous Fox Glacier with the New Zealands most experienced and oldest glacier guides. This is for climbing up the upper part with the addition  of 2 helicopter flights. This trip is suitable for novices and experts but please have a good fitness level. The average price is $599. Equipment is provided but book quickly.
