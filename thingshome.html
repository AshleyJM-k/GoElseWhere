With two islands, dozens of unique cities, goldent beachs, rolling mountians and 14 national parks there is plenty of activities and attractions to choose from. Looking for a few highlights and suggestions? You are in the right place!


Top attractions and activities

Not sure where to start your adventure? Here are our top 2 location picks


Christchurch

Come visit the largest city in the South Island filled with adventure for any art lover with museums and galleries or the nature lover with parks, rolling hills and riverboats.



Glow Worm Caves

Glow worm caves, or Waitomo Caves is an amazing cave labyrinth of stalagmites, stalacitites and what seems to be galazies of mysterious fireflies that light up the caves in a fantasy like way. Take a night time hike, boat trip or black water rafting to experince this fully



Other activities



Stay awhile...

Camping in New Zealand is a popular local pastime being known as the Land of the untouched outdoors. Stay awhile and enjoy sitting around a campfire on a clear-skied evening and listening to the waves or the breeze through the mountian trees.



On a different type of road...

Hiking is one of the best ways to completely explore and fullfill your adventure to New Zealand while taking in the views of the beautiful landscapes of rugged coastlines, farmland, river valleys, towering forests and larger than life mountians.



Wine and Dine

CReady to rest and refresh after a long day or are more of an urban explorer? New Zealand has ample amounts of diverse culture when it comes to their foods. Take a look at some of our most unique choices.
