New Zealand Freedom Camping laws.


Essential points of the law on caming is
Any person of legal standing has the right to freedom of movement in any public space in New Zealand. This is also enshrined in New Zealands Bill of rights.
Any person has the right to park overnight in any public space
Anyone parking in a public space overnight is bound by the same laws as occupying public land at any time, anotherwords respect for the environment, not causing damage, littering or improperly disposing of water.
Local counciles have certian powers to restrict or prohibit freedom caming in specific areas and will be signed
