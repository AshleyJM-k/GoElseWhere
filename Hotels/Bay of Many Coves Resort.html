About this hotel

Overlooking the Bay of Many Coves, this upscale waterfront resort is 2 km from Ruakaka Bay and 8 km from Punga Cove Resort.

Plush, airy apartments with floor-to-ceiling windows have free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and kitchenettes, plus balconies with bay views. Upgraded apartments add extra bedrooms and laundry facilities. A luxe 2-bedroom suite has a complimentary minibar, plus a fireplace, and a furnished deck with a hot tub. Room service is available.

Amenities include a heated outdoor pool, a hot tub and a day spa, plus a helipad and a jetty. There's a casual eatery, a fine-dining restaurant and a bayside cafe. Kayaks and boats are available.

Check-in time: 2:00 PM
Check-out time: 10:00 AM
