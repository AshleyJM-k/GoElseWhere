Glow-worm Caves

The glow worm caves, or the Waitomo Caves. This cave is unique to the country and even the world. You can take a boat ride ungergrown in the caves to experience the thousand of glowing tiny creatures that light up the cave roof above you, making it look like an almost fantasy like stary sky.Glow worm caves are rich in Maori history, myths and legends. If you are not one for caves you can also see these glowing blue-green worms in damp, overgrown places such as banks of lakes, rivers and forests. Guided night time kayacking and hiking trips are offered are the best way to experince the magical views above ground. This is a great quick weekend activity that isnt too far from cities. Enjoy cafes and walks as well around the Marokopa Falls and limestone Mangapohue Natural bridge.
